OUR HISTORY

Humps Soccer Academy

Humps Soccer Academy was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate soccer enthusiasts who shared a vision of creating a world-class football development program for young players. The academy's humble beginnings can be traced back to a small training ground in the heart of the city, where a handful of dedicated coaches and players gathered to chase their dreams.
